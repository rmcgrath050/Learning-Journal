## Session Aims & Objectives


By the end of today you'll have working code, real evidence for your portfolio, and one thing you can change at work this week:

-Defend your pipeline's SLA - explain why your data delivery agreements exist and what they actually protect

- Run a data risk register that actually gets used - spot, log and report the nine ingestion risks that break pipelines in the real world

- Handle PII without creating tomorrow's incident - apply real strategies for classifying, minimising and protecting sensitive data at ingestion

- Catch quality issues before they reach the warehouse - evidence the practices that stop bad data poisoning your reports

- Write Python that supports your organisation's net-zero target - compress ingested data and prove the carbon win in code
